Hotkeys and Shortcuts

Hotkeys​

Open luna window: command/control + E

Scene Hotkeys​

Open selected scene: shift + ctrl/command + click on scene

Select or deselect all scenes: shift + click on scene

Select only one scene: ctrl/command + click on scene

Pick active scene: alt/option + click on scene

Build hotkeys​

Build only active scene: command/control + shift + alt/option + E

Build project: command/control + shift + E

Shortcuts​

Show asset in editor: double click on any asset or folder

Open CS errors: double click on any error
